Quick start
1. Scan. Tap "Start scanning," point your camera at a bottle's barcode. Known bottles just get a name/quantity to confirm.
2. Going fast? Turn on Quick Capture. Known bottles tick up silently; unknown ones get snapped so you can keep moving instead of stopping to type.
3. Label later. Captured photos land in "To label" below — review them whenever, with a name guess pre-filled for you to check.
4. No barcode match? Type the name once, manually or in the review queue — that barcode is remembered for good after that, for you and everyone else using this tool.
